### 公式推导

#### (2.12) -> (2.13)

$$
\begin{align}
& E\_{Y|X}([Y-c]^2 | X=x)  \\\\
= & E\_{Y|X}(Y^2 - 2cY + c^2 | x)  \\\\
= & E\_{Y|X}(Y^2 | x) - E\_{Y|X}(2cY | x) + c^2  \\\\
\end{align}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial E\_{Y|X}([Y-c]^2 | X=x)}{\partial c} = 2c - 2E\_{Y|X}(Y|X = x) = 0
\end{align}
$$

$$
\therefore f(x) = c = E\_{Y|X}(Y|X=x)
$$
